Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Myanmar Korean Dictionary ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web service (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account or sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Email Address: Used for account identification and communication
  • Display Name: Your name as provided by Google Sign-In
  • Profile Picture: Your profile picture from Google (if available)

1.2 Profile Information

To provide personalized features, we may collect:

  • Nationality: To understand our user demographics
  • Birthdate: For age-appropriate content and analytics
  • Korean Language Level: To customize learning content
  • Position/Job Title: For analytics and content personalization
  • Gender: For demographic analytics

1.3 User-Generated Content

When you use our Service, we collect:

  • Word Submissions: New words you submit for review
  • Report Forms: Requests for new categories, words, or translation error reports
  • Bookmarks: Words you save to your library
  • Search History: Words you search for (stored locally on your device)

1.4 Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use our Service:

  • Device information (platform, OS version)
  • App usage statistics (features used, time spent)
  • Error logs and crash reports

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Service Provision: To provide, maintain, and improve our dictionary service
  • User Authentication: To verify your identity and manage your account
  • Content Personalization: To customize your learning experience based on your Korean level and preferences
  • Word Review: To review and approve user-submitted words and reports
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our Service and improve it
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries and provide support
  • Points System: To track and reward your contributions (word submissions, reports)

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, which provides:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication via Firebase Authentication
  • Firestore database for structured data storage
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

3.2 Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you wish to delete your account, please contact us at edu.mmkr@gmail.com.

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google Sign-In

We use Google Sign-In for authentication. When you sign in with Google, Google shares your basic profile information (name, email, profile picture) with us. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

4.2 Firebase Services

We use Google Firebase for:

  • Firebase Authentication: Secure user authentication
  • Cloud Firestore: Database storage for user data, word submissions, and reports

Firebase's use of your information is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.3 Text-to-Speech

Our app uses device-native text-to-speech functionality to pronounce Korean words. This processing happens locally on your device and does not send audio data to our servers.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your profile information through the app
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-Out: Stop using the Service and delete your account at any time
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us at edu.mmkr@gmail.com.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from those in your country. By using our Service, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: edu.mmkr@gmail.com
App Name: Myanmar Korean Dictionary
Website: https://mm-krdictionary.vercel.app
